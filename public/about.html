About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2010, The Continental has grown to become a leading name in luxury hospitality across the globe. We are committed to providing unforgettable experiences with first-class service, dining, and accommodations. Our journey has been one of innovation, excellence, and a passion for hospitality.

Mission & Values

Our mission is simple: to provide luxurious and memorable experiences for every guest. We stand by these values:

  • Commitment to Quality
  • Innovation and Creativity
  • Customer-Centric Approach
  • Sustainability and Responsibility

Our Team

At The Continental, we pride ourselves on having a team that is passionate about hospitality. From chefs to housekeeping, each member plays a vital role in delivering exceptional service to our guests.

Our Locations

We have expanded globally to bring our luxury experience to New York, Paris, and Osaka. Each of our locations is designed to offer guests a unique blend of local culture with exceptional service and top-tier amenities.

New York, Manhattan

Our Manhattan location offers stunning views of the skyline and an unparalleled experience in the heart of New York City.

Paris

Located in the vibrant city of Paris, our hotel blends French elegance with modern luxury, offering spectacular views of the Eiffel Tower.

Osaka

Our Osaka location offers a unique blend of Japanese tradition and contemporary luxury, located near the bustling city center.

Whether you're planning a business trip or a leisurely getaway, we invite you to experience the finest in luxury across our global locations.
